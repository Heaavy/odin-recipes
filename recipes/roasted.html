Oven-Roasted Chicken Thighs!

Try my oven-grilled chicken thighs and you will fall in love!
Sprinkle with fresh finely chopped parsley and serve with a warm potato salad, or fries, or both.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup dark soy sauce
  • ¼ cup Thai sweet chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 8 (5 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken thighs

Directions/Instructions

  1. Step

    Whisk soy sauce, chili sauce, paprika, parsley, and pepper together in a large glass or ceramic bowl.
    chicken thighs and toss to evenly coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator for up to 1 hour.

    Step

    Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and place a wire rack on the tray.

    Step

    Remove chicken thighs from the marinade and shake off excess; transfer to the prepared rack. Discard the remaining marinade.

    Step

    Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Turn and continue to bake until no longer pink in the centers and juices run clear,
    10 to 15 minutes more. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
